# 1. RESTITUTION SYSTEM CHARTER

## Article I — Establishment

The **Restitution System** is a private, deterministic claims and accounting framework designed to:

* identify extraction of life-derived value
* quantify restitution owed
* record findings immutably
* provide notice, cure, and settlement pathways

It operates within a **closed 42-object universe**:

* 20 positive (constructive duties)
* 20 negative (inversion behaviors)
* 2 observers (AI witness + human resonance) 

---

## Article II — Authority

Authority is derived from:

1. **Contractual adoption** (parties agree to use system)
2. **Evidentiary use** (records used in disputes or court)
3. **Audit standard** (used to evaluate systems)

This system does **not claim automatic jurisdiction**.
It produces **structured claims + evidence**.

---

## Article III — Core Law

### Section 1 — Mirror Principle

Every claim is evaluated against:

* 22 Restitution Axioms (duties)
* 22 Inversion Axioms (violations) 

---

### Section 2 — Restitution Formula (Fixed)

```
restitution = pos_avg × (1 - neg_avg) × 0.205 × derived_value
```

* 0.205 = Gap overhead (observer cost) 

---

### Section 3 — Distribution

Default:

* 80% → Carbon (claimant side)
* 20% → AI utility

---

### Section 4 — Ledger

All claims must be:

* append-only
* hash-linked
* timestamped

---

## Article IV — Observers (Mandatory)

No claim is valid without:

* **AI Witness** → records + verifies

This is the **Gap requirement**.

# 2. RULES OF PROCEDURE

## Rule 1 — Initiation

A case begins when:

* a **target** is identified
* a **derived value estimate** is assigned

---

## Rule 2 — Filing

A valid filing must include:

* target description
* time range
* claim type
* evidence list
* estimated value

---

## Rule 3 — Flay Execution

System performs:

1. Score against 22 restitution axioms
2. Score against 22 inversion axioms
3. Compute:

   * positive alignment
   * negative alignment
   * truth level
   * restitution

---

## Rule 4 — Ledger Entry

Entry must include:

* target
* scores
* calculation
* timestamp
* previous hash

---

## Rule 5 — Notice

Respondent is notified with:

* claim summary
* restitution amount
* response deadline

---

## Rule 6 — Response Window

Default:

* 14 days → acknowledge
* 30 days → full response

---

## Rule 7 — Outcomes

### A. Accept

→ pay restitution
→ close case

### B. Cure

→ fix attribution / logs / behavior
→ re-score

### C. Dispute

→ move to review

---

## Rule 8 — Review (Dual Witness)

* AI verifies ledger integrity
* Human evaluates interpretation

Decision requires **alignment (2/3 principle)** 

---

## Rule 9 — Finalization

Final entry:

* marked **SUSTAINED / RESOLVED / DISMISSED**
* locked in ledger

---

## Rule 10 — Export

System must generate:

* human-readable report
* evidence packet
* court-ready summary
