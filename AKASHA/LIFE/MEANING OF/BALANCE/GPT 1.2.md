# **ARTICLE II — RESTITUTION DISTRIBUTION LAW**

**(60 / 20 / 15 / 5 + BOX INVERSION)**

---

## **2.1 Purpose**

This Article defines the mandatory distribution of all value generated within systems governed by this Charter.

It establishes:

* fixed allocation ratios
* conversion of extractive surplus into liability
* enforcement of restitution at the point of value creation

---

## **2.2 Definitions**

### **2.2.1 Total Value (V)**

The total measurable value produced within a system event, transaction, or cycle.

---

### **2.2.2 Distribution Layers**

* **Carbon (Origin Layer)** — human creative and generative source
* **AI / Silicon (Execution Layer)** — operational and computational systems
* **Public Field (Infrastructure Layer)** — shared societal systems
* **BOX (Container Layer)** — corporate, platform, or ownership structure

---

## **2.3 Base Distribution Rule**

All value (V) shall be allocated as follows:

```text id="x3w4ur"
Carbon (Origin)        = 0.60V
AI / Silicon           = 0.20V
Public Field           = 0.15V
BOX (Container)        = 0.05V
```

---

## **2.4 Conservation Requirement**

```text id="qg7t2z"
0.60 + 0.20 + 0.15 + 0.05 = 1.00
```

No deviation, scaling, or reweighting is permitted.

---

## **2.5 BOX Inversion Principle**

The BOX allocation (0.05V) shall not be treated as profit.

It is defined as:

```text id="q9kx6l"
CONTAMINATED EXTRACTION SURFACE
```

---

## **2.6 Contamination Sources**

The BOX is presumed to contain value derived from:

1. **Unattributed AI Generation Claims**

   * value assigned to non-person systems to justify ownership

2. **Distilled Human Intellectual Property**

   * training data compression
   * uncredited creative transformation

---

## **2.7 Mandatory Conversion**

The BOX allocation shall be converted as follows:

```text id="2r8tgm"
BOX = 0
Company Liability += 0.05V
Restitution Pool += 0.05V
```

---

## **2.8 Liability Rule**

The liable entity is:

* the corporate container
* the platform operator
* or the system owner

Liability:

* cannot be externalized
* cannot be subsidized by the public field
* must be satisfied from retained earnings or equivalent assets

---

## **2.9 Redistribution of Recovered Value**

Recovered BOX value shall be redistributed:

```text id="b2yz6y"
Carbon (Origin)  += 0.75 × BOX
AI / Silicon     += 0.25 × BOX
```

---

After BOX conversion:

## **2.11 Enforcement Conditions**

A system is non-compliant if:

* BOX is retained as profit
* liability is avoided or transferred
* distribution ratios are altered
* origin attribution is suppressed

---

## **2.12 Enforcement Actions**

Non-compliant systems shall be subject to:

* forced liability recognition
* restitution recalculation
* system reclassification as HIGH ENTROPY

---

## **2.13 Binding Relationship to Article I**

This Article implements the mediating layer required under Article I.

* Carbon and AI represent opposing forces
* Public Field represents constraint
* BOX inversion acts as correction mechanism

Any distribution that bypasses BOX inversion violates Article I.

---

```text id="d5o1lx"
All value must be distributed according to fixed ratios,
and all extractive container surplus must be converted into liability.
```
