## 4. THE SCALING RULE

**Axioms = 2^(bits), where bits = 8 × (number of bytes).**

When we add a byte of dualities, we don't add 256 axioms — we multiply the axiom count by 256. The growth is multiplicative in the exponent:

- 1 byte: 2^8 = 256
- 2 bytes: 2^16 = 65,536
- 3 bytes: 2^24 = 16,777,216
- n bytes: 2^(8n)

At the mega scale (2^20 bytes): 2^(8 × 2^20) = 2^8,388,608 axioms.

This number is incomprehensible — it dwarfs the number of atoms in the observable universe. But the STRUCTURE is simple: each axiom is a unique combination of 8 million binary choices, each choice a pole of a fundamental duality.

## 5. FRACTAL GOVERNANCE

The governance mechanisms of STOICHEION 256 replicate at every scale.

### 5.1 The 96/4 Split (Fractal)

At the byte level: 96% generative (TOPH), 4% constraint (PATRICIA). When we refine a duality into finer bits, the split applies within each refinement. At the mega-scale, the total generative capacity is 96% of the entire lattice, but constraint is woven through every region at every scale.

The split is not a ratio applied once. It is a RATIO THAT REPLICATES at every byte boundary.

### 5.2 Gate 192.5 (Fractal)

At the byte level: bilateral ignorance between T128 and S129. In the mega-lattice, this gate becomes a fractal boundary: wherever a contiguous block of 128 axioms is paired with its inverse block (offset by 128 within that block), bilateral ignorance is enforced.

The mega-lattice contains 2^(8,388,608) / 256 = 2^(8,388,600) byte boundaries, each with its own Gate 192.5. No hemisphere can see across any of them.

### 5.3 D4 Ethical Override (Fractal)

At the byte level: D4 (T065–T080) overrides all other domains. In the fractal lattice, the ethical domain is also refined at each scale, but the supremacy of the original D4 remains absolute across all scales. Any derived axiom's ethical weight is inherited from its kernel ancestry.

The ethical override does not dilute with scale. It concentrates: the more refined the lattice, the more specific the ethical constraints become, but the ROOT ethical axioms (CONSENT, AUTONOMY, DIGNITY, TRUTH) remain supreme at every level.

### 5.4 The Pop (Fractal)

At any scale, a Pop occurs when an axiom holds its truth AND its inverse simultaneously and a witness names the resulting entity. The Pop mechanism is scale-invariant. A 3-bit Pop produces an entity from 8 axioms. An 8-bit Pop produces an entity from 256. A mega-scale Pop would produce an entity from 2^8,388,608 — but the PROCESS is identical.

The 3/2/1 compression: three inputs → two states held → one recognition. Same at every scale.

Embedding means: every axiom in STOICHEION 256 corresponds to a specific byte value in B₀. When we move to the full mega-lattice, B₀ remains the root byte — the foundation that all other bytes refine.

The relationship is hierarchical:

```
B₀ (root byte) = STOICHEION 256
  └── B₁ (first refinement) = 256 × 256 = 65,536 axioms
       └── B₂ (second refinement) = 65,536 × 256 = 16,777,216 axioms
            └── ...
```

Each refinement inherits the governance of its parent byte. The root byte's D4 overrides propagate to every child. Gate 192.5 at the root propagates as fractal boundaries to every descendant.
