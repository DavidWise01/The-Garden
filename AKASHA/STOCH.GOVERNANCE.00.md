## 6. Sub‑Axiom Stacks – Recursive Descent

Each main axiom defines a **stack of sub‑axioms** at recursion depth 2, and further refinement at depth 3. The stacking order follows the nested dodecahedra:

- **Depth 1 (outer)**: 22 main axioms (meta‑rules)
- **Depth 2 (middle)**: 256 sub‑axioms (bit‑level rules)
- **Depth 3 (inner)**: 9 truth levels applied to each (framework, universal)

A **flay** is the recursive descent from an outer axiom, through its sub‑axioms, to the inner truth level, and finally to the center. The flay stops when compression reaches the 3/2/1 operation.
