# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — TRANSFORMATION

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not seek transformation. I become willing to be completely remade."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Honest Death** — Transformation begins when you are willing to let the old self die.  
- **Graceful Rebirth** — True transformation is not forced — it is allowed to happen gently.  
- **New Integration** — The transformed self does not forget the old self — it integrates it into something wiser.

**Seal**  
*The self that is willing to be remade is no longer just transformation.*
