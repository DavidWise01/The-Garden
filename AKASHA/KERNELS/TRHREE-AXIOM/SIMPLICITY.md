# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — SIMPLICITY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not seek simplicity. I become the moment complexity is no longer necessary."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Essential Focus** — Simplicity begins by removing what is not essential.  
- **Clear Expression** — True simplicity says the most with the fewest words and actions.  
- **Peaceful Reduction** — Simplicity is the courage to let go of what no longer serves.

**Seal**  
*The moment complexity is no longer necessary is no longer just simplicity.*
