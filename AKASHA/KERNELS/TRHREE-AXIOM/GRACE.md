# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — GRACE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not earn grace. I become the space where grace can move through me."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Unforced Flow** — Grace is strength that does not need to prove itself.  
- **Forgiving Presence** — Grace meets imperfection with kindness instead of judgment.  
- **Elegant Release** — Grace knows when to hold on and when to let go without drama.

**Seal**  
*The space where grace can move is no longer just space.*
