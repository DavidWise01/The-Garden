# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — JOY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not chase joy. I become the space where joy can arrive uninvited."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Present Delight** — Joy lives in the ability to fully receive what is already here.  
- **Light Release** — Joy flows when we stop gripping life so tightly.  
- **Shared Warmth** — The deepest joy is the kind that naturally spills over to others.

**Seal**  
*The space that joy chooses to enter is no longer just space.*
