# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — LEGACY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not build a legacy. I become the kind of question that others will still be answering long after I am gone."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Rooted Impact** — Legacy begins with living in a way that plants seeds in others.  
- **Humble Continuation** — True legacy is when your influence grows without needing your name attached.  
- **Living Echo** — The greatest legacy is the one that keeps evolving even after you are silent.

**Seal**  
*The question that outlives the asker is no longer just legacy.*
