# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — PERSEVERANCE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not quit when it gets hard. I become the reason it was worth continuing."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Rooted Commitment** — Perseverance is anchored in remembering why you started.  
- **Daily Return** — Perseverance is showing up again even when yesterday was difficult.  
- **Quiet Strength** — True perseverance does not announce itself — it simply continues.

**Seal**  
*The reason that keeps you going is no longer just perseverance.*
