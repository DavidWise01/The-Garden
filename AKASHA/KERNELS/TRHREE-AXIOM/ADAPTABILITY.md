# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — ADAPTABILITY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not resist change. I become the change while still remembering who I am."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Fluid Strength** — Adaptability is strong enough to bend without breaking.  
- **Wise Release** — True adaptability knows what to keep and what to let go.  
- **Creative Response** — Adaptability does not just survive change — it creates something new from it.

**Seal**  
*The form that can change shape without losing its essence is no longer just adaptability.*
