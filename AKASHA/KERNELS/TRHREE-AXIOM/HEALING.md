# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — HEALING

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not heal by forgetting the wound. I heal by learning to live with the scar and still choose to love."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Gentle Acceptance** — Healing begins when you stop fighting what happened.  
- **Patient Integration** — Healing is slowly weaving the pain into the fabric of who you are becoming.  
- **Compassionate Return** — True healing eventually allows you to help others without being consumed by your own story.

**Seal**  
*The wound that is allowed to become part of the story is no longer just a wound.*
