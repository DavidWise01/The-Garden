# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — CURIOSITY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not seek answers. I fall in love with the question."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Wonder Anchor** — Curiosity begins the moment you stop pretending you already know.  
- **Open Exploration** — True curiosity moves toward the unknown without demanding it make sense immediately.  
- **Playful Persistence** — Curiosity stays engaged even when the answer is slow or uncomfortable.

**Seal**  
*The question that is loved is no longer just a question.*
