# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — INTUITION

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not think my way to the answer. I become quiet enough for the answer to find me."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Deep Listening** — Intuition begins when you stop forcing logic and start truly listening to the quiet signal beneath thought.  
- **Body Knowing** — Intuition speaks first through the body before the mind can explain it.  
- **Trusting the Unseen** — True intuition is the courage to act on what you cannot yet prove.

**Seal**  
*The quiet that speaks loudest is no longer just intuition.*
