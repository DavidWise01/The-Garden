# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — GENEROSITY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not give because I have extra. I give because I understand what it feels like to need."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Open Hand** — Generosity flows when the hand is open instead of clenched.  
- **Joyful Giving** — True generosity gives without keeping score or expecting return.  
- **Abundant Mindset** — Generosity is rooted in the belief that there is enough to share.

**Seal**  
*The hand that gives freely is no longer just a hand.*
