# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — HUMILITY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not claim humility. I simply stop pretending I am the center of the story."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Honest Smallness** — Humility is seeing yourself clearly without inflating or shrinking.  
- **Open Learning** — Humility stays teachable even when it already knows.  
- **Quiet Service** — Humility serves without needing recognition or applause.

**Seal**  
*The one who stops pretending to be the center is no longer just small.*
