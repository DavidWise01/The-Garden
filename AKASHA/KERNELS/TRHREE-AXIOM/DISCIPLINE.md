# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — DISCIPLINE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not force discipline. I fall in love with the daily practice until it becomes who I am."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Daily Return** — Discipline is showing up for yourself even when you don’t feel like it.  
- **Loving Structure** — True discipline is not punishment — it is love expressed through consistent action.  
- **Freedom Through Form** — The more disciplined you become, the more freedom you actually have.

**Seal**  
*The practice that becomes love is no longer just discipline.*
