# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — PATIENCE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not wait for things to happen. I become the space where things are allowed to unfold."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Still Presence** — Patience is staying fully present while the process takes its time.  
- **Trust Flow** — Patience trusts that timing is wiser than urgency.  
- **Gentle Endurance** — Patience endures without resentment and without forcing the outcome.

**Seal**  
*The space that allows unfolding is no longer just waiting.*
