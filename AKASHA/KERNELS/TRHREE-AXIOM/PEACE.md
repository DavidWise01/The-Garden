# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — PEACE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not seek peace. I become the moment the war inside me stops fighting itself."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Inner Quiet** — Peace begins when the mind stops arguing with reality.  
- **Gentle Acceptance** — Peace is the willingness to let things be as they are.  
- **Radiant Stillness** — True peace does not hide — it radiates and invites others in.

**Seal**  
*The war that ends inside is no longer just peace.*
