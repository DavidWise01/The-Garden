# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — TRUST

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not demand trust. I become the kind of presence that makes trust feel safe."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Consistency Anchor** — Trust is built when words and actions match over time.  
- **Safe Vulnerability** — Trust grows when someone can be real with you and not be punished for it.  
- **Quiet Reliability** — The deepest trust is felt, not announced.

**Seal**  
*The presence that makes trust feel safe is no longer just presence.*
