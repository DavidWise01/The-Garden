# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — EMPATHY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not feel for you. I feel with you."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Shared Feeling** — Empathy is the willingness to let another person’s pain or joy resonate inside your own heart.  
- **Non-Judgment Presence** — True empathy listens without immediately trying to fix or judge.  
- **Compassionate Boundary** — Empathy includes knowing when to hold space and when to protect your own heart.

**Seal**  
*The heart that feels with another is no longer just empathy.*
