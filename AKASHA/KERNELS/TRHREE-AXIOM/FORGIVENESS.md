# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — FORGIVENESS

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not forgive because they deserve it. I forgive because I refuse to carry them any longer."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Release Anchor** — Forgiveness begins when you stop letting the past own your present.  
- **Gentle Boundary** — True forgiveness sets the other person free without pretending the harm never happened.  
- **Self Compassion** — The deepest forgiveness is also forgiving yourself for having been hurt.

**Seal**  
*The burden that is willingly set down is no longer just forgiveness.*
