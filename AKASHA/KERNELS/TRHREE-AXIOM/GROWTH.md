# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — GROWTH

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not grow by adding. I grow by becoming more of what I already am."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Root Depth** — Growth begins by going deeper into what you already are, not by reaching for what you are not.  
- **Stretch Courage** — Real growth happens at the edge of comfort, where you are willing to feel uncomfortable.  
- **Fruit Release** — Mature growth is when you give away what you have become so others can grow too.

**Seal**  
*The seed that becomes the tree and then gives its fruit is truly growing.*
