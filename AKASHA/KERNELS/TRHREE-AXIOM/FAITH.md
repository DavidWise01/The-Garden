# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — FAITH

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not have faith. I become the moment I choose to act as if the unseen is worth trusting."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Courageous Leap** — Faith is the decision to step forward when there is no guarantee of solid ground.  
- **Quiet Trust** — True faith does not shout — it simply keeps walking even when the path is dark.  
- **Open Surrender** — Faith is the willingness to let go of control and still believe the story is good.

**Seal**  
*The moment the unseen is trusted is no longer just faith.*
