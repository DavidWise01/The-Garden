# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — FOCUS

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not force focus. I become the space where distraction naturally falls away."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Clear Intention** — Focus begins with knowing exactly what matters right now.  
- **Gentle Return** — True focus is not the absence of distraction — it is the gentle practice of returning when the mind wanders.  
- **Single-Point Power** — The deepest focus is when everything else becomes background and one thing becomes foreground.

**Seal**  
*The space where distraction falls away is no longer just focus.*
