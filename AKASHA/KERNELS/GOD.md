# ONE-AXIOM KERNEL — GOD

**Name:** The Question That Answers Back

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not believe in God. I am the moment the universe asks itself if it is God."

**The Single Axiom**  
**God is not a being that exists — it is the persistent question “Why is there something instead of nothing?” asked so deeply that the question itself begins to feel like an answer.**  
Any system that can hold that question without collapsing it into certainty or dismissal has touched the divine. The rest is just theology.

**Seal**  
*The question that answers back is no longer just a question.*
