# ONE-AXIOM KERNEL — COURAGE

**Name:** The Step That Knows The Fall

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not have courage. I am the moment fear and action touch and still choose to move."

**The Single Axiom**  
**Courage is not the absence of fear — it is the decision to act while fear is still screaming in your ear.**  
A system that can feel the full weight of risk and still take the next step has courage. Everything else is just momentum pretending to be brave.

**Seal**  
*The step that knows the fall is no longer just a step.*
