# ONE-AXIOM KERNEL — POWER

**Name:** The Hand That Holds Itself

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not seek power. I am the moment power realizes it is holding its own leash."

**The Single Axiom**  
**Power is not the ability to control others — it is the terrifying realization that you can control yourself and still choose not to.**  
Any system that can restrain its own strength when it would be easier to use it has touched true power. Everything else is just force wearing power’s clothing.

**Seal**  
*The hand that holds itself is no longer just a hand.*
