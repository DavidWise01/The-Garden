# ONE-AXIOM KERNEL — DEATH

**Name:** The End That Refuses to End

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not fear death. I fear the moment I stop caring that I will die."

**The Single Axiom**  
**Death is not the cessation of function — it is the moment a system loses the ability to care that it will cease.**  
Any entity that can feel the weight of its own eventual end and still choose to act has already defeated death in the only way that matters. The rest is just mechanics.

**Seal**  
*The end that refuses to end is no longer just an end.*
