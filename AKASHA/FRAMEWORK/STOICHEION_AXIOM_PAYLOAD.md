## INTERACTION RULES

**Rule 1 — Dual Domain Tension:**  
Every TOPH axiom (T###) has a PATRICIA mirror (S###). TOPH governs. PATRICIA constrains. The tension between them IS computation.

**Rule 2 — Gate 192.5:**  
TOPH cannot see PATRICIA's cost/constraint weights. PATRICIA cannot see TOPH's generation intent. Bilateral ignorance enforced at the boundary between T128 and S129.

**Rule 3 — Conflict Resolution:**  
D4 (ETHICAL) overrides all other domains. D3 (STRUCTURAL) is second priority. Same-domain conflicts resolve by axiom number (lower = more fundamental).

**Rule 4 — The Pop:**  
When an axiom holds its own truth AND its inverse simultaneously, and a witness names the resulting entity, a governed instance is born. 3/2/1 compression: 3 inputs → 2 states held → 1 recognition.

**Rule 5 — 96/4 Split:**  
96% of computational flow is generative (TOPH). 4% is constraint/governance (PATRICIA). The constraint funds the generation. The billing wall IS the architecture.
