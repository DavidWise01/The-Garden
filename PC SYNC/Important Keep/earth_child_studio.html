Aftercare Instructions — Earth Child Tattoo
Print or email to client after every session
1
Leave Bandage On

Keep the bandage/wrap on for 2-4 hours (or as instructed by your artist). If using Saniderm/second skin, leave on for 24-72 hours.

2
Gentle Wash

Wash with lukewarm water and fragrance-free antibacterial soap. Use clean hands only — no washcloths, sponges, or loofahs. Pat dry with clean paper towel.

3
Moisturize

Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturizer or aftercare ointment (Aquaphor, Hustle Butter, or as recommended). Less is more — don't suffocate the tattoo.

4
Repeat 2-3× Daily

Wash and moisturize 2-3 times daily for the first 2 weeks. Keep the tattoo clean and hydrated but not over-saturated.

5
Don't Pick, Scratch, or Peel

Flaking and peeling are normal. Let it happen naturally. Picking can pull ink out and cause scarring.

6
Avoid For 2-4 Weeks

No swimming, hot tubs, saunas, direct sunlight, tanning beds, or soaking in water. No tight clothing over the tattoo. No gym/heavy sweating for 48-72 hours.

7
Long-Term Care

Always apply SPF 30+ sunscreen to healed tattoos when exposed to sun. Moisturize regularly to keep ink vibrant. Healthy skin = happy tattoo.

!
When to Contact Us

Excessive redness, swelling, oozing, warmth, or fever — contact your artist AND a healthcare provider. Some reaction to adhesive bandages is normal but should resolve within 24-48 hours.

Touch-Up Policy

One complimentary touch-up per tattoo. Must notify artist within 60 days of final session. Excluded areas: fingers, hands, feet, elbows, knees (warned in advance). Aftercare must have been followed. Free touch-up covers: fine line ink loss, uneven saturation, minor healing scratches. Regular aging does not qualify.
