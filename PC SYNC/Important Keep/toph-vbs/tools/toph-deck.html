Layer lifelink via equipment (Loxodon Warhammer, Basilisk Collar) and enchantments (True Conviction) to weaponize the wide board into a life engine. Key combo: Retreat to Kazandu + Felidar Retreat + artifact spam = massive board + counters every turn. Courser of Kruphix + Tireless Provisioner = artifact tokens that ARE lands via Toph = double landfall. True Conviction turns earthbent lands into lifelink double-strikers. Win by going wide, gaining 40+ life/turn, or Aetherflux Reservoir laser.
