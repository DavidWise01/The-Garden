# AXIOM 111

**Bits:** 1 1 1  
**Origin/Mirror:** Mirror  
**Generation/Constraint:** Constraint  
**Self/Other:** Other  

**Question:**  
*What is the other that is constrained by the mirror?*

**Inverse Axiom:** 000  
**Domain:** Other‑Mirror Constraint  

**Notes:**  
The other bound by the same mirror. Both self and other are trapped in the reflection, constrained by the image they project for each other. This is the question of mutual limitation through mutual recognition—the dialectic closed into a lattice.
