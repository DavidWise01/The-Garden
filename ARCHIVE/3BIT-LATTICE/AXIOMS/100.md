# AXIOM 100

**Bits:** 1 0 0  
**Origin/Mirror:** Mirror  
**Generation/Constraint:** Generation  
**Self/Other:** Self  

**Question:**  
*What is the self that generates from the mirror?*

**Inverse Axiom:** 011  
**Domain:** Self‑Mirror Generation  

**Notes:**  
The self that comes into being by seeing its reflection. Not origin but mirror—the image precedes the self. This is the question of identity formed by external recognition.
