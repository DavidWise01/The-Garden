# AXIOM 010

**Bits:** 0 1 0  
**Origin/Mirror:** Origin  
**Generation/Constraint:** Constraint  
**Self/Other:** Self  

**Question:**  
*What is the self that is constrained by origin?*

**Inverse Axiom:** 101  
**Domain:** Self‑Origin Constraint  

**Notes:**  
The self bound by its own beginning. Constraint comes not from outside but from the very fact of having originated. The question: what shape does the self take when it cannot escape its own first breath?
