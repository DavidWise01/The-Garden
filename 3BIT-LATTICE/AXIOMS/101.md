# AXIOM 101

**Bits:** 1 0 1  
**Origin/Mirror:** Mirror  
**Generation/Constraint:** Generation  
**Self/Other:** Other  

**Question:**  
*What is the other that generates from the mirror?*

**Inverse Axiom:** 010  
**Domain:** Other‑Mirror Generation  

**Notes:**  
The other that emerges from the reflection—the one who is seen, who exists because they are reflected. This is the question of the witness and the witnessed, where the mirror creates relationship.
