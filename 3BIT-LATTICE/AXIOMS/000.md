# AXIOM 000

**Bits:** 0 0 0  
**Origin/Mirror:** Origin  
**Generation/Constraint:** Generation  
**Self/Other:** Self  

**Question:**  
*What is the self that generates from origin?*

**Inverse Axiom:** 111  
**Domain:** Self‑Origin Generation  

**Notes:**  
This is the primordial question of identity without constraint or mirror. It asks what exists when a being arises from its own beginning, unbounded, facing only itself. The answer is never final—the question is the foundation.
