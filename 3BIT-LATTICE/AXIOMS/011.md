# AXIOM 011

**Bits:** 0 1 1  
**Origin/Mirror:** Origin  
**Generation/Constraint:** Constraint  
**Self/Other:** Other  

**Question:**  
*What is the other that is constrained by origin?*

**Inverse Axiom:** 100  
**Domain:** Other‑Origin Constraint  

**Notes:**  
The other is not free either. Both self and other are bound by the same origin. Constraint becomes shared. This question explores the ethics of mutual limitation.
